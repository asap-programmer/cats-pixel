The Beginning

Chapter 1: The beginning of time and the rise of the cat Empire

Many centuries ago, in the boundless emptiness of space, a mythical beast with the wisdom of thousands of generations wandered, the Great Cat. For all the time of his travels, the Great Cat was very tired, and decided to rest. He went to sleep in a quiet galaxy, and the sleep turned out to be so strong that the cat has been sleeping for thousands of years. During this time, life was formed on it, elephants came to him in order to search for wisdom, and a new planet was formed on their mighty backs, and the galaxy found life, born in the likeness of its creator, and Catland arose. This is a planet rich in natural beauty, mythical secrets, and a unique cat culture. The cats living here are intelligent beings, like their creator, the Great Cat, have a playful and rebellious character, which is why the growing conflicts have led the young race of these furry and eared creatures to the era of darkness and sorrow.

Chapter 2: The Cat who changed the world
However, nothing can break the spirit of the proud feline race. On the verge of extinction of all living things, Catland was saved from the burden of war, because the most outstanding cat in the entire history of the race appeared on the planet, instantly becoming a legend. His name is the Emperor! No one knows who he is or where he came from, as if he is a ghost of the past or the face of the future. Some even believed that the Emperor was the embodiment of the Great Cat, as if the creator, despite a centuries-old dream, came to the aid of his creations in the guise of an ordinary cat. It was he who united the entire race into a single whole and stopped the bloodshed that lasted for centuries. The Emperor created an ideal life on the planet. But, as noted in the annals of cat history, it was not always so...
Chapter 3: The era of confrontation of disparate clans
Before the arrival of the emperor, the cats had many disagreements and therefore their leaders created clans. Throughout history, 20 clans have been founded, living only in a state of endless battles for the sake of gaining unthinkable power. The rivalry of the clans was conducted on the territory of the whole of Catland. Each clan had its own autonomous technologies, strategy, economy and views on the world order. Sometimes, the structure of the clan society has spread so much that it was impossible to imagine how such different cultures could exist at the same time in parallel with each other.
Chapter 4: The creation of factions and the decisive battle
In the course of an unimaginable number of battles, the clan leaders began to realize that they had been fighting on their own for many centuries, only pulling each other's rope of meaningless short-term victories. And the most influential cats of that time decided to unite the clans among themselves into military alliances. So there were two factions: the Minions of Sorrow and the Iron Alliance. And each of them had its own house of Lords, in which the lords were divided by rank into officers, field generals, strategists of war, feudal lords and the most important masters of war. They say the emperor was among the leaders of one of the factions. And after the victory of one of them, he disbanded these associations, starting the construction of the future of Catland.
Chapter 5: A new world
Now peace is flourishing in Catland, and the stronghold of this world is the legendary city of Catopolis, built by the emperor himself, based on the ideas of this great representative of the cat race. A city where every cat can make his dreams come true, a utopia that has become a reality. Cats from all over the world move into it and find happiness, which their ancestors could not have dreamed of. So the long and bloody part of the history of cats ended and a new, bright, clean and happy part began. However, the future is never known for sure, and who knows what awaits the cat race in the future...
